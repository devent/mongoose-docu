\label{par:kill}
\parparameter{\parcommand{kill} [\partype{sigspec} | \partype{signum},] \partype{pid} | \partype{object}}

Send the signal named by \partype{sigspec} or \partype{signum} to the
processes named by \partype{pid} or \partype{object}. \partype{sigspec} is
either a case-insensitive signal name such as \code{SIGKILL} or a signal
number; \partype{signum} is a signal number. If \partype{sigspec} is not
present, then \code{SIGTERM} is assumed.
